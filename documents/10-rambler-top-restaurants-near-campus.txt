Top 9 Restaurants Near Georgia Tech in Atlanta Midtown (Updated 2025)
By Sydnie
Atlanta stands out as a culinary haven, boasting one of the nation’s finest food scenes. The areas around Georgia Tech and Tech Square serve as hotspots for a diverse range of dining experiences just a stone’s throw from campus.
For students seeking top-notch eateries in Atlanta Midtown, we’ve curated this article spotlighting nine of the best, student-approved restaurants near campus. From quick and casual dining options to sit-down establishments and late-night bites, we’ve got you covered in this carefully crafted list.
9 Best Restaurants Near Georgia Tech’s Campus (Updated 2025)
Fast Casual
1. Atwoods
Address: 817 W Peachtree St NW
Distance from campus: 1 minute walk
Photo Courtesy of Atwoods Pizza.
Enjoy Neapolitan-inspired pizza at Atwoods Pizza Café in Atlanta, where their menu centers around expertly crafted pies cooked to perfection in an Italian Pavesi Oven with a rotating floor, ensuring a consistently delightful dining experience with each order. With a focus on quality ingredients and precision in their cooking process, Atwoods promises pizza enthusiasts a taste of excellence in every bite.
2. RReal Tacos
Address: 100 6th St NE #110
Distance from campus: 6 minute walk
Photo Courtesy of Rreal Taco.
Indulge in the true essence of Mexican street food at Rreal Tacos, a restaurant dedicated to crafting authentic flavors from various regions of Mexico. From delectable tacos and cheesy quesadillas to expertly crafted margaritas, Rreal Tacos invites you to savor the freshest cuts of meat and ingredients sourced exclusively from Mexico, ensuring an unparalleled dining experience.
3. Superica
Address: 99 Krog St NE
Distance from campus: 12 minute walk
Photo Courtesy from Supericca.
Experience the vibrant world of Tex-Mex brought to life at Superica in Atlanta, where chef and restaurateur Ford Fry channels the rich history of this flavorful cuisine born in the 1940s. With a menu inspired by the robust flavors of Texas ranchers and traditional Northern Mexican cuisine, Superica offers a delightful array of craveable classics, from Dirty Breakfast Tacos to Grilled Shrimp Diablo, ensuring a Tex-Mex feast that’s heavy on flavor and light on fuss.
Sit-Down Restaurants
1. Ray’s New York Pizza
Address: 26 5th St NW
Distance from campus: 1 minute walk
Photo Courtesy of Ray’s New York Pizza.
Discover the irresistible charm of Ray’s New York Pizza in Atlanta, a beloved establishment since 1990, celebrated for its delicious pizza crafted with the freshest ingredients. With a commitment to excellence, Ray’s offers a diverse menu featuring hand-tossed pizzas, enticing entrees and vegetarian options, creating an inviting atmosphere where patrons can unwind with a full bar, handcrafted cocktails and plenty of TVs for sports enthusiasts, making it the go-to spot for quality food, drinks and entertainment.
2. Cypress Street Pint & Plate
Address: 817 W Peachtree St NW
Distance from campus: 3 minute walk
Photo Courtesy of Cypress Street.
Step into the heart of Midtown Atlanta and experience the nostalgic charm of Cypress Street Pint & Plate. Boasting a diverse menu featuring snacks, salads, burgers, sandwiches, sweets, sides and Midtown favorites, along with a robust selection of beers on tap, bottled options and a variety of wines and spirits, Cypress has established itself as a neighborhood staple, inviting patrons to savor a classic and satisfying dining experience.
3. Antico Pizza
Address: 1093 Hemphill Ave NW
Distance from campus: 6 minute walk
Photo Courtesy of Experience Avalon.
Enjoy some of the best pizza Atlanta has to offer at Antico Pizza. A distinguished Michelin Bib Gourmand winner for 2023, Antico Pizza Renowned for their unwavering commitment to crafting exceptional Neapolitan pizzas. With unique pizza flavors such as Lasagna pizza and a prime location near West Campus, Antico is a popular weekend dinner spot for Georgia Tech students.
Late Night Eats
1. Waffle House
Address: 66 5th St NW
Distance from campus: 1 minute walk
Nestled in Tech Square, Waffle House offers a quintessential Southern dining experience, dishing out iconic waffles, hearty breakfast classics and a welcoming atmosphere that has made it a beloved Atlanta institution. From the sizzle of the grill to the warm aroma of freshly brewed coffee, the Waffle House in Tech Square continues to serve as a cherished spot for students embodying the spirit of comfort food and Southern hospitality in the heart of Atlanta, 24 hours a day.
2. Insomnia Cookies
Address: 930 Spring St NW
Distance from Campus: 5 minute walk
Photo Courtesy of Insomnia Cookies.
Indulge your sweet tooth at Insomnia Cookies in Tech Square, where the aroma of freshly baked treats fills the air. Offering a delectable assortment of warm cookies, brownies and ice cream, Insomnia Cookies has become a delightful destination for those seeking a sugary pick-me-up in the heart of Atlanta. What makes this spot even sweeter are the late night hours, perfect for your next midnight treat.
3. Taco Mac
Address: 66 5th St NW
Distance from campus: 10 minute walk
Photo Courtesy of Eater Atlanta.
For a late-night dinner of wings, burgers and fries, stop in at the Taco Mac in Midtown. Open until 2:00 AM, this sports grill features beer of the month, weekly pint nights and all of your bar food favorites. Whether you’re up late watching a sports game on their plethora of TVs or looking for a post-concert meal, Taco Mac is sure to hit the spot.
